About me

Hi, my name is Stavros. Instead of including a picture of myself in my 'about me'section, I thought it would be better PR to put up a picture of my cat Gordon.

I am a UI/UX Designer with a background in visual art. I currently work at Graphika, a disinformation analytics company (yes, unfortunately fake news is a thing). Before starting web design and falling down the rabbit hole into UX work, I was a professional illustrator and carved, amongst other things, portraits onto tombstones for an Uzbeki family-owned headstone company in Brooklyn. I also illustrated for magazines, did the odd graphic design assignment, and art handled for various galleries across the city.

These days, when I'm not working or trying to code, I play music in a local Brooklyn band, write songs for a new album, and watch NBA basketball because it's great.
